    <h1>How we treat your home's information.</h1>                                                                                    
    <div class="meta">Last updated: 2026-05-06</div>                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                      
    <p>Nestara is a private space for your household. This page explains what we collect, what we don't, and how to take your data    
  with you.</p>                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                      
    <h2>What we store</h2>                                  
    <p>We store only what your household needs to coordinate:</p>
    <ul>                                                                                                                              
      <li><strong>Account</strong> — email, display name, profile photo (if you provide one), and the device tokens we use to deliver
  notifications.</li>                                                                                                                 
      <li><strong>Circle data</strong> — events, tasks, lists, shopping items, recognition (points, streaks, badges), and the activity
   feed within your circle.</li>                                                                                                      
      <li><strong>Email preferences</strong> — your weekly-email day, time, timezone, and which sections you want included.</li>
    </ul>                                                                                                                             
    <p>Your circle's data is visible only to members of that circle and to Nestara's backend.</p>                                     
                                                                                                                                      
    <h2>What we don't do</h2>                                                                                                         
    <ul>                                                                                                                              
      <li>We don't sell your information.</li>              
      <li>We don't share it with advertisers.</li>
      <li>We don't use your data to train external models.</li>
      <li>We don't read the contents of your linked Apple or Google Calendar beyond what's needed to render events you've explicitly  
  chosen to import.</li>                                                                                                              
    </ul>                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                      
    <h2>Third parties we depend on</h2>                     
    <p>To run the app we use:</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Firebase</strong> (Google) — authentication, Firestore database, file storage, Cloud Functions, and Cloud Messaging
  for notifications. Subject to <a href="https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy">Firebase's privacy terms</a>.</li>              
      <li><strong>Resend</strong> — sending the weekly summary email. Subject to <a 
  href="https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy">Resend's privacy policy</a>.</li>                                                    
      <li><strong>Apple</strong> (EventKit) — only when you explicitly link an Apple calendar.</li>
      <li><strong>Google Calendar API</strong> — only when you explicitly link a Google calendar.</li>                                
    </ul>                                                                                                                             
    <p>We don't share any data with these providers beyond what's strictly needed for the feature you've turned on.</p>
                                                                                                                                      
    <h2>Children</h2>                                       
    <p>Nestara is rated 4+ and is designed for family use. We don't market the app to children or collect data targeted at children.  
  Parents within a circle control who else joins.</p>                                                                                 
   
    <h2>Notifications and email</h2>                                                                                                  
    <p>You can turn each notification category on or off from <strong>Profile → App settings → Notifications</strong>. You can change
  or pause the weekly email from <strong>Profile → Weekly email</strong>, or unsubscribe from any weekly email's footer link.</p>     
   
    <h2>Your data, your circle</h2>                                                                                                   
    <p>You can leave a circle at any time from <strong>Profile → Circle</strong>. When the last member leaves a circle, the circle's
  data is removed. Direct deletion or export requests can be sent to the email below.</p>
