Nestara is a small, calm app, but if anything stops working — a chore that won't complete, an email that didn't arrive, or a feature that's confusing — we want to hear about it.

Reach us

Email is the fastest way:

Nestaraapp@gmail.com

Tell us what you were doing, what you expected, and what happened instead. A screenshot helps. We usually reply within a couple of days.

Common questions

  • I'm not getting the weekly email. Check Profile → Weekly email; make sure it's on, the time is right, and the timezone matches yours. Also check your spam folder.
  • A chore won't mark complete. Tasks can only be completed on the day they're due. Future days show a dashed circle to indicate "scheduled, not active".
  • I want to leave my circle. Profile → Circle → tap your name → Leave circle.
